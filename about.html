    Charis Berakha Limited engages in ,ICT equipment Printing, and supplies of Engineering equipment, Technical Training equipment, and Laboratory equipment, Workshop Equipment, General supplies and other related supplies in the Engineering Field. The firm is privately owned by a number of directors whom wealth of experience in the ICT, Engineering and Related fields in diverse industry sectors has driven the company's exponential growth to its current successful levels.
